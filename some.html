INSTAGRAM

Instagram is a social media platform with over a billion active users. It has also been the main sharing platform for other campaigns and movements, which has shown to make an impact. In addition, it is also free to use and easy for DWMY to convey its message. This lets us share our tips in the form of videos and pictures while also reaching out to a large group of people in all different ages. By being on this platform and showing off, DWME can inspire and engage the youngsters of Instagram to care about energy.









TIK TOK

TikTok is a new and exciting app with young users, exactly the age group we would like to reach out to. After all, the world's future is depending on gen z. DWME believes that by conveying our message on TikTok can contribute to making gen z care more about the environment, spread awareness and contribute to better energy for all.







TWITTER

Twitter is an excellent place to share opinions and news, but also tips and tricks for DWME. It allows us to reach out to all kinds of age groups along with an older audience that does not necessarily have TikTok or Instagram. Like the other platforms, DWMY is also here to spread awareness and engage everyone to think about how they chose to use their energy.
